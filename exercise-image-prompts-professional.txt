GLOBAL ART DIRECTION (restated inside every brief below)
  Medium: Strictly a FLAT-COLOR 2D VECTOR ILLUSTRATION, in the style of a
    modern fitness-app icon set. This is NOT a 3D render, NOT a
    photorealistic image, NOT a painterly or airbrushed image, and NOT a
    photograph. Bold clean black or dark-gray outlines around every shape.
    Flat cel-shading only — at most one single soft shadow tone per surface
    (e.g. one shade darker on the underside of an arm), no gradients, no
    photographic skin texture, no specular highlights, no realistic
    lighting/shadow falloff. If the result looks like a photo, a 3D render,
    or a painting rather than a clean flat icon-style illustration, it is
    wrong — regenerate flatter and simpler.
  Character: One fixed, reusable mascot design, rendered IDENTICALLY across
    every panel and every exercise in this library — do not let any
    attribute drift between images:
      - Hair: short cropped dark-brown hair, textured crop style, no facial
        hair, no hat.
      - Skin tone: a warm light-to-medium tan/beige skin tone (mid-range
        olive-beige — not pale/white, not deeply tanned, not ruddy).
        Render this exact tone flatly, with no photographic shading
        variation across the body.
      - Build: athletic and lean, broad-shouldered but NOT
        bodybuilder-extreme or exaggerated — a fitness-app "everyman"
        proportion, not a hyper-muscular comic-book physique.
      - Face: simple, calm, neutral expression, minimal facial detail (dot
        or simple-line eyes, small nose, no visible eyebrow emphasis) —
        keep the face plain and iconic rather than detailed or photoreal.
      - Age: appears late 20s to early 30s.
      - Wardrobe (identical in every image): a fitted heather-gray
        crew-neck athletic tank top OR a fitted navy tank top (pick one and
        keep it consistent across the whole set if possible), solid dark
        navy athletic shorts of mid-thigh length, solid dark navy/blue
        low-top athletic sneakers with white soles.
    Treat the above as a locked character sheet, not a loose suggestion —
    the goal is for every exercise image in the library to look like it
    was illustrated by the same artist using the same character template.
  Composition: Three horizontal panels of equal width, arranged side by side
    and separated by thin gray divider lines, combined into a single image
    with an overall wide aspect ratio of approximately 2:1 (reference: an
    existing image set at 1774x887px).
  Background: Plain light gray/off-white. For machine movements, render the
    equipment itself with accurate mechanical detail, as it is functionally
    central to the exercise.
  Sequence: Each panel depicts one phase of a single repetition — start,
    mid-range, and end — unless otherwise noted.
  Exclusions: No on-image text, no logos, no watermarks, no captions.
